**Project:** UNWIND  
**Version:** Draft v0.1 (for legal review)  
**Last updated:** 2026-03-01 (Europe/London)

> ⚠️ **Important:** This is a project draft for policy alignment. It is not legal advice and must be reviewed by qualified counsel before production use.

---

## 1) Purpose

This Contributor License Agreement ("CLA") allows contributions to the UNWIND project to be:

- distributed in the open-core codebase,
- maintained and relicensed where necessary for project sustainability,
- used in commercial/premium offerings.

This CLA does not remove contributor attribution in project history.

---

## 2) Definitions

- **Project**: UNWIND and its official repositories and documentation.
- **Maintainer**: The legal entity or individual operating UNWIND repositories and releases.
- **Contribution**: Any code, docs, tests, scripts, designs, or related material intentionally submitted to the Project.
- **You**: The individual contributor, or authorized representative of an organization submitting contributions.

---

## 3) Copyright ownership

You retain ownership of your original contribution(s).

By signing this CLA, you grant Maintainer the rights in Sections 4–5 for contributions submitted by you.

---

## 4) Copyright license grant

You grant Maintainer a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable license to:

1. use, reproduce, modify, and prepare derivative works of your contributions,
2. publicly display and distribute your contributions,
3. sublicense and relicense your contributions (including under proprietary/commercial terms),
4. combine your contributions with other software and materials.

---

## 5) Patent license grant

To the extent you hold patent rights that would be infringed by your contribution as submitted, you grant Maintainer and downstream users a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free patent license to make, use, sell, offer for sale, import, and otherwise transfer implementations incorporating your contribution.

If you initiate patent litigation asserting that the Project or a contribution infringes your patents, this patent grant terminates for you as of filing date.

---

## 6) Representations

You represent that:

1. you have the legal right to submit the contribution,
2. the contribution is your original work or you otherwise have rights to submit it,
3. to your knowledge, the contribution does not knowingly violate third-party IP rights,
4. you are not intentionally including confidential or unauthorized proprietary material.

If contributing on behalf of an organization, you represent you are authorized to bind that organization.

---

## 7) Outbound licensing

The Project may distribute your contribution under AGPL-3.0 for open-core releases and may also distribute under other licenses for commercial offerings.

---

## 8) No obligation / no warranty

- This CLA does not obligate Maintainer to accept, use, or continue using any contribution.
- Contributions are provided "as is" without warranties unless separately agreed.

---

## 9) General

- This CLA applies to all past and future contributions submitted by you to official UNWIND repositories unless superseded by a later signed agreement.
- If any clause is unenforceable, remaining clauses remain in effect.
