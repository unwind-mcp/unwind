# Oracle Cloud Feedback

**Subject: 7 hours to discover your "free" tier doesn't actually exist**

---

Your website states it takes "2-6 minutes to create a VM instance." I'd like to report that my actual experience was closer to 7 hours — from 8pm to nearly 2am on a Friday night — and I still don't have a running instance.

I signed up for Oracle Cloud's Always Free tier specifically for the VM.Standard.A1.Flex Ampere instance (4 OCPUs, 24 GB RAM) that you prominently advertise. After completing registration, email verification, and adding my credit card, I spent the entire evening attempting to create this instance. Every single availability domain in UK South (London) — AD-1, AD-2, and AD-3 — returned "Out of capacity" errors. Every. Single. One.

At no point during the sign-up process did Oracle mention that the advertised free tier resources might not actually be available. There is no waitlist. There is no estimated availability date. There is no notification system to alert me when capacity frees up. You just let people sign up, fill in their card details, and then hit a wall.

When I attempted to upgrade to Pay As You Go — thinking that paying customers might get priority — the upgrade form repeatedly failed with a generic "Something went wrong while trying to upgrade your account" error. I tried this four times. No useful error message. No diagnostic information. Just failure.

So to summarise my experience:

- Your "2-6 minutes" claim is, for the London region at least, completely fictitious
- Your Always Free tier advertises resources that are not available to provision
- Your PAYG upgrade process is broken
- Your UI required me to navigate a 4-step wizard repeatedly, with the shape selection silently changing when switching availability domains
- 7 hours of my evening wasted, with nothing to show for it

If capacity is not available in a region, say so on the sign-up page. Don't let people waste hours of their lives discovering it the hard way at 2am.

A deeply frustrated would-be customer,
David Russell
